The daughter I had always dreamt of having. My light and my motivation.

Elizabeth, the heartbeat of our home, a whirlwind of joy, laughter, and boundless curiosity. With energy that seems to never run out, she moves through life with a sense of wonder and excitement that's truly contagious. Whether she's twirling in the living room or asking a hundred thoughtful questions before bed, every moment with her feels like a new adventure. Her smile doesn't just light up the room, it fills it with warmth, turning even the dullest days into something bright and unforgettable.

What makes her truly special, though, is her heart. She has a kindness that seems to come from somewhere deeper than her years should allow. She's the first to share, the first to comfort, and the first to make someone laugh when they need it most. Her hugs are pure magic, her words are full of empathy, and her soul shines with a goodness that can't be taught, it's just who she is. She is a light in this world, and I feel endlessly grateful to witness her grow, love, and live so fully each day.
